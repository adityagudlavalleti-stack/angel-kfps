Angel is a KFPS-registered Friesian stallion born January 16, 2024, in Ixtapan Tonatico, Estado de México. Awarded the prestigious Vb H · Premium classification — the highest distinction in the KFPS studbook — Angel represents the gold standard of Friesian breeding. His jet-black coat carries no markings, and his movement reflects the commanding power and elegant carriage the breed is renowned for. Bred by Luis Francisco Angeles Rodriguez, Angel is the product of elite bloodlines on both sides and is available for stud services across Mexico and internationally.

Angel carries the genetics of some of the most distinguished Friesian bloodlines in the KFPS registry.
